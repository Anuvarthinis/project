FIT AND FINE




Fitness Tips

Benefits of Regular Exercise

  • Improved cardiovascular health
  • Increased muscle strength
  • Weight management
  • Enhanced flexibility

Sample Workout Routines

  1. Warm-up: 5 minutes of light cardio
  2. Strength Training: 3 sets of squats, push-ups, and planks
  3. Cardio: 20 minutes of jogging or cycling
  4. Cool-down: 5 minutes of stretching

Nutrition Tips

  • Consume a balanced diet rich in fruits and vegetables.
  • Stay hydrated throughout the day.
  • Avoid excessive sugar and processed foods.
  • Include lean protein sources in your meals.

Exercise List

Here's a list of exercises for a full-body workout:

  1. Push-ups: 3 sets of 12 reps
  2. Squats: 3 sets of 15 reps
  3. Planks: Hold for 30 seconds, repeat 3 times
  4. Jumping jacks: 3 sets of 20 reps
  5. Lunges: 3 sets of 12 reps per leg
  6. Mountain climbers: 3 sets of 20 reps per leg
  7. High knees: 3 sets of 30 seconds
  8. Burpees: 3 sets of 10 reps

Make sure to warm up before starting your workout and cool down afterward with stretching exercises.
