Plank Exercise

A simple yet effective core strengthening exercise

Instructions

The plank is a core-strengthening exercise. To perform a basic plank:

  1. Start in a push-up position with your arms straight and your wrists, elbows, and shoulders in alignment.
  2. Engage your core muscles and hold your body in a straight line from head to heels.
  3. Hold this position for as long as you can while maintaining good form.

Variations

There are several variations of the plank exercise, including:

  • Side Plank
  • Forearm Plank
  • Plank with Leg Lifts
  • Plank with Shoulder Taps

Benefits

The plank exercise offers various benefits:

  • Strengthens core muscles, including abs, lower back, and obliques.
  • Improves posture and reduces the risk of back pain.
  • Enhances overall stability and balance.
  • Requires no equipment and can be done anywhere.
